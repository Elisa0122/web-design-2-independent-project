Condominium Overview

The condominium was built in the early 1980s. The houses were built by the Minto company. There are currently 175 houses. There are several kinds of houses: with garage, without garage, with 3 floors or 2 floors and a basement. The area is well served by buses and in 2025 it will be served by O-train.

Property Manager Company

Our property manager is Scott Smith of Condominium Management Group. We've been working with him for 40 years. Please contact Scott if you have any concerns or maintenance requests. He can be reached by email at ssmith@condogroup.ca or by phone at 613 237 9519x227

Useful Information

Visitor parking

Our village is patrolled by Response Security. Vehicles parked in a visitor's space overnight (between 2:00 AM and 7:00 AM) must be registered. You can register your guest's vehicle online or by calling 613 820 3657 or 613 820 3657.

If a vehicle is illegally parked in your assigned parking space, contact City of Ottawa By-Law Services directly at 311.

Vehicles can be registered for up to 3 nights. Unregistered vehicles will be ticketed or towed at owner’s expense.

Garbage & Recycling

The City of Ottawa offers a weekly garbage pick-up service for compost. The collection of cardboard, plastic, and waste is done alternately bi-weekly.

In Castlebrook Village, the garbage are collected on Wednesdays.
