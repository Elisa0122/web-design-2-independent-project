Board Overview

Our condominium corporation is governed by a 5-member board of directors. The board approves all condo rules, projects, and budgets, and has engaged a professional property manager to handle the day-to-day operation of our village. Board members are volunteers elected at the Annual General Meeting held every Fall, and serve for a two-year term. The board and property manager officially meet ten times each year and communicate via email to discuss issues as they arise.
